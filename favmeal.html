Fried Rice

Fried rice is a really lovely meal thats easy to make and tastes raelly good. Although it requires a lot of ingredients to prepare it, its really very easy.

Ingredients used Qty
rice 2-3cups
green beans 100g
green peas 100g
carrots 100g
green pepper 100g
curry 1 teaspoon
chicken 1kilo
thyme 1 teaspoon

  1. Wash your rice
  2. Wash and properly dice your greens
  3. Wash and steam your protein until its tender
  4. Procced to fry your protein
  5. Cook your washed rice with the stock gotten from your protein until its almost tender, add salt and seasoning to taste
  6. In a pan, stir fry your veggies with curry, thyme and any seasoning of your choice
  7. Pour the stir fried veggies into the pre cooked rice and mix properly.
  8. Laeve it on heat for at least 15mins without covering
  9. Your Fried rice is Ready
  10. Serve alongside your protein and a chilled drink
  11. Enjoy
